The Procurement Ecosystem

How managed procurement transforms long-term care operations.

National Tier Pricing Optimization for Skilled Nursing Facilities

Par Level AI optimizes national tier pricing within major distribution portals — giving individual SNFs access to pricing structures typically reserved for multi-facility hospital networks. Our procurement engine maps every SKU to the lowest available contract tier, eliminating the markup gap that single-facility operators absorb.

Streamlining Long-Term Care Supply Chains Beyond Traditional Broker Models

Traditional supply chain brokers negotiate contracts but don't execute procurement. Par Level AI replaces fragmented broker relationships with a fully managed model — handling every purchase order, vendor interaction, and formulary update. The result: fewer vendors, consolidated invoicing, and pricing that doesn't depend on a middleman's margin.

Automated Per Patient Day Tracking for SNF Administrators

Per Patient Day (PPD) is the metric that drives every procurement decision in skilled nursing. Par Level AI automates PPD tracking across all supply categories — Medical, Dietary, Janitorial, Linens — correlating spend against daily census to surface cost anomalies before they reach month-end reporting. Administrators get category-level visibility without manual reconciliation.
